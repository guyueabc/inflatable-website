====================================================
  HOW TO ADD YOUR PORTFOLIO IMAGES
====================================================

Just put your photos in this folder with these exact names:

  01.jpg  →  Giant Inflatable Mascot
  02.jpg  →  Event Archway
  03.jpg  →  Product Replica
  04.jpg  →  Custom Character
  05.jpg  →  Trade Show Display
  06.jpg  →  Holiday Decoration

FORMAT: JPG, PNG, or WebP
SIZE:   Recommended 800x600 pixels or larger
        Any aspect ratio works — images will crop to fit

RENAMING: Right-click each image → Rename → type "01.jpg", "02.jpg", etc.

After adding images, refresh the browser (F5) to see them on the page.
